## 🎨 DESIGN HIGHLIGHTS

Visual Style:
✨ Gradient backgrounds (blue/purple/pink)
✨ Card-based layouts throughout
✨ Hover effects (scale, shadow, color)
✨ Modern typography with clear hierarchy
✨ Color-coded category cards
✨ Lucide-react icons everywhere
✨ Badge components for labels

Color Scheme:
🔵 Primary: Blue 600
🟣 Secondary: Purple 600
🟠 Accent: Pink/Orange for variety
⚪ Backgrounds: White, Slate 50, Slate 900
📊 Gradients: Blue → Purple → Pink

Components:
├─ shadcn/ui Button (3 variants)
├─ shadcn/ui Card (with headers)
├─ shadcn/ui Badge (2 variants)
└─ Lucide icons (20+ different icons)
